Welcome to Cookie Walla!

Our Mission is to create a one-of-a-kind experience for each of our guests. We believe that good food and a warm ambiance can bring people together and create unforgettable moments. That's why our cafe is the perfect place to meet old friends or make new ones.

Cookie Walla is more than just a cafe, we have a special story. We have been proudly serving customers for 13 years, starting our journey in the amazing country of Nepal. Over time, we became a popular destination where everyone could enjoy great food and wonderful company.
